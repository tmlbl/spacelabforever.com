Bio

"New age Jimi, mixed with one part Basquiat. Original. Rolando de la vega, Leader of the true school."

Climbing out out f the punk era as jungle emerged out of reggae, Ro stood tall as a figure of influence among many of our favorite music makers early introductions to the craft. During the early development of the dubstep movement that grew out of underground jungle in the mid to late 1990s, Rolando enlisted in the Army in 1999 where he served in a combat role for over ten years making periodic stops in Hawaii, Panama, London, Spain, NYC, Chicago, and Nashville maintaining impactful relationships and has been herald as a privilege to those who fortunate enough to experiecnce his offering during his leaves.
